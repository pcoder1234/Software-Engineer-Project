Software Engineering

Welcome to Software Engineering!

Today we will explain to you what software engineering is and everything you should know about it.

Salary Range

Software engineers make from $80k for entry-level engineers, to as much as $270k per year for experienced software engineers

Education Requirements

Software engineers require at least a bachelor's degree in software engineering or computer science

Advancement Opportunities

Software engineers usually start out as junior engineers, then become senior engineers. After that, they have many options, including becoming a tech manager, or the end goal for many software engineers, a Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

Responsibilities

Software engineers build computer programs including but not limited to websites, video games, and more! They are responsible for making sure their programs are high-quality and they work.

We hope you learned a lot about software engineering and how you could make it your career!
